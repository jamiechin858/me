    2022 SD County Fair Submission

    I created this collage in my Design & Mixed Media 1-2 high school course, and submitted it to be displayed at the 2022 San Diego County Fair in the Del Mar Fairgrounds. This piece incorporates different mediums: acrylic paint, charcoal, watercolor, and jelly print. I also used magazines, bubble wrap, a beaded necklace, a headband, and printed craft paper. This piece represents growth from hardships. I used pieces of magazines that were printed with water because I was inspired by one of my favorite quotes: “a smooth sea never made a skilled sailor” - Franklin D Roosevelt. The textures of the different items I added helps to portray the roughness of difficult times.
